ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Dan Mu. I am currently a student from the University of California San Diego, majoring in computer science. My goal is to become a software developer. In the past, I led the team to do research on software verification through gamification in order to improve the correctness of the software verification system. I have also worked in a startup company called UiLink, developing an app that can help students find housing as well as roommates. I enjoy approaching complex projects and desire to build an understanding of the customer’s needs. Have a look at the information below, and get in touch to learn more.
